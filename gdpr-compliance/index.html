Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

This agreement governs the processing of personal data by TechNexus on behalf of its customers, ensuring full compliance with GDPR and international data protection standards.

policy

GDPR Certified

Our infrastructure is audited annually against EU-GDPR standards, ensuring that data sovereignty and user rights are maintained at the highest level of technical sophistication.

Security

End-to-end encryption for data at rest and in transit (AES-256 / TLS 1.3).

1. Roles and Responsibilities

The parties acknowledge that for the purposes of the GDPR, the Customer acts as the Data Controller and TechNexus acts as the Data Processor. TechNexus shall process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Customer, including with regard to transfers of personal data to a third country or an international organization.

Data Controller

  • Determines purposes and means of processing
  • Ensures legal basis for data collection
  • Manages data subject requests directly

Data Processor

  • Processes data on behalf of Controller
  • Implements robust technical security
  • Maintains sub-processor transparency

2. Technical and Organizational Measures

TechNexus has implemented and will maintain throughout the term of the Agreement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect Personal Data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure, or access.

  • Pseudonymization: Data is hashed or masked where possible during non-production cycles.
  • Availability: Redundant systems across multiple availability zones ensure 99.99% uptime.
  • Restoration: Regular backups are tested weekly for integrity and recovery speed.

Continuous Monitoring

Real-time threat detection and automated compliance reporting.

TechNexus shall, to the extent legally permitted, promptly notify Customer if it receives a request from a Data Subject to exercise their rights (e.g., access, rectification, erasure). We provide tools within our console to help you respond to these requests instantly.
