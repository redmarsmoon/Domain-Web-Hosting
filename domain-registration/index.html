Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to register a domain? expand_more

Registration is instantaneous. Once payment is confirmed, your domain is secured immediately. However, it may take 24-48 hours for global DNS propagation to complete before your website becomes visible worldwide.

What is WHOIS Privacy Protection? expand_more

Every domain registration requires the registrant's contact information to be added to the public WHOIS database. Privacy protection replaces your personal details (name, email, phone) with our generic proxy information, keeping you safe from spam and unwanted solicitations.

Can I transfer my domain later? expand_more

Yes, ICANN rules allow you to transfer your domain to any registrar of your choice after 60 days from the initial registration or a previous transfer.
