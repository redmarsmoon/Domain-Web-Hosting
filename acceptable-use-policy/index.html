Acceptable Use Policy

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the rules and prohibited practices for using the TechNexus infrastructure network and related services. Our goal is to ensure a secure, reliable, and high-performance environment for all users.

Restricted Activities

1. Network & Infrastructure Security

  • cancelUnauthorized attempts to circumvent security measures, probe vulnerabilities, or perform unauthorized penetration testing on any TechNexus system or network.
  • cancelLaunching or facilitating Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks, flooding, or any activity intended to degrade the performance of the network.
  • cancelInterfering with the service of any user, host, or network including, without limitation, via means of submitting a virus, worm, or Trojan horse.

2. Illegal & Fraudulent Conduct

  • cancelTransmitting, distributing, or storing material in violation of any applicable law or regulation, including copyright, trademark, or trade secret laws.
  • cancelEngaging in phishing, spoofing, or any activity meant to deceive users or harvest sensitive information under false pretenses.

3. Resource Abuse

  • cancelExecuting resource-intensive automated scripts or processes that exceed the normal usage patterns and negatively impact other tenants.
  • cancelUtilizing our infrastructure for unauthorized cryptomining activities without explicit written consent from TechNexus.

Enforcement

Failure to comply with this AUP may result in immediate action to protect our users and network integrity.

Level 1: Notification

A formal warning detailing the violation and required corrective measures within 24 hours.

Level 2: Restriction

Temporary suspension of specific ports, IP ranges, or API endpoints related to the violation.

Level 3: Termination

Immediate account termination for repeated violations or malicious intent without refund.
